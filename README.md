**#CHROME EXTENSION**
This chrome extension named RedWedding enhances the user experience while using chrome.

**Features**
As the name suggest the extension has a red and black theme. This extension also generates random background image and random quotes when we open a new tab. This extension also provides current timer which is places in middle of a screen and pomodoro timer in top right corner.

**Usage**

- Enjoy refreshing background images of your choice
- Keep track of the time with current time displsyed right at the centre.
- Get motivated and inspired by the quotes generated every time you open or refresh tab.
- Enhance your productivity with pododoro timer and enjo the animation of the timer.
- Lastly indulge into a red and black theme and bring passion and intensity into your work.

**Links**
For generating random background images : https://source.unsplash.com/random/1920x1080/?modern.

For generating random quotes : https://api.quotable.io/random.
